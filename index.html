Exploring the World of Coffee Culture

Despite data having a selection of only 55 countries, a major demand for coffee across these countries is present in 2019-2020 alone, therefore the influence that coffee has throughout the world is a force to be reckoned with.

Different countries have their own popular selection of coffee beans that they prefer, notably 2 different kinds being Arabica and Robusta. Arabica is known for its smooth, sugary taste that is aromatic. However, Robusta is known for its strong, bitter taste that is high in caffeine.
